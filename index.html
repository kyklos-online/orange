Orange Game

Welcome to Orange Game

How To Play

  • Hand: Click‑drag the orange (or a slice). Release → the object is thrown in the direction of the drag.
  • Sword: Click the orange to split it into slices. Click a slice again to re‑join it (removes the slice).
  • Black Hole: When enabled it pulls the orange and its slices toward its centre.
  • Hoop: Pass the orange through the hoop to earn points; the hoop teleports after each score.
  • Skins: Reach 100 points → Lemon skin unlocks; 500 points → Grape skin unlocks. Select a skin from the dropdown.
  • Gold orange: 50 pts are awarded when you slice a gold orange (instead of 10).
  • Reset: Clears saved progress.
