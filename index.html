How To Play

  • Unsliced: The Orange and its skins start whole and visible.
  • Hand: Click and drag the orange, its slices, or any block. While a block is selected you can rotate it with ← / → keys and press ↓ to reset its rotation.
  • Sword: Click the orange or a slice to slice it into smaller pieces (orange disappears). Click a slice again with the sword to recombine the slices back into the orange.
  • Racket / Power Racket: Click near objects to push them.
  • Delete: Remove a block by clicking it.
  • Draw: Draw on the orange while holding the mouse.
  • Black Hole: Toggle to attract the orange and its slices strongly, and importantly the blocks also attract orange and slices slightly. You can drag the black hole.
  • Loop: Makes the orange (and slices) move continuously side‑to‑side.
  • Reset: Restores the initial state (clears saved game).
  • Control key: Press to hide/show all tools.
  • Tab key: Opens a side tab with extra shape blocks (rectangle, square, diamond, triangle, slope). Click a shape to add it to the play area.
  • Arrow keys: Rotate selected block left/right, ↓ resets rotation.
  • Hoop: Toggle the hoop. When the orange passes through the hoop you earn points; the hoop teleports to a new location after each score.
  • Points: 100 points → Lemon skin (click enabled at 100 points), 500 points → Grape skin (click enabled at 500 points), 1000 points → Necklace + superspeed & supersize (togglable).
  • Throwing: When you release a dragged orange or slice, it is thrown in the direction of your last drag.
  • Player 2 controls: Use WASD keys to move the orange slightly.
  • Goal: There is a left-side hoop (player 1 goal) and right-side goal (player 2); scoring adds points respectively.
  • Each round: Timer 40 seconds, auto toggles Power Racket and normal gravity, scores updated.
