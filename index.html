How To Play

  • Hand: Click and drag the orange or any slice. While dragging the orange you can move it anywhere, including upward.
  • Sword: Click the orange to slice it into smaller pieces (or make it disappear).
  • Racket / Power Racket: Click near objects to push them.
  • Black Hole: Toggle to attract the orange and its slices.
  • Loop: Makes the orange (and slices) move continuously side‑to‑side.
  • Reset: Restores the initial state.
  • Control key: Press to hide/show all tools.
  • Hoop: Toggle the hoop. When the orange or a slice passes through the hoop you earn points; the hoop teleports after each score.
  • Points: 100 points → Lemon skin, 500 points → Grape skin, 1000 points → Necklace + superspeed & supersize.
