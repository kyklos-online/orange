How To Play

  • Hand: Click‑drag the orange (or a slice). Release → the object is thrown in the drag direction.
  • Sword: Click the orange to split it into slices. Click a slice again to re‑join it (removes the slice).
  • Black Hole: When active it pulls the orange and all slices toward its centre.
  • Hoop: Pass the orange through the hoop to earn points; the hoop relocates after each score.
  • Skins: Reach 100 pts → Lemon skin unlocks; 500 pts → Grape skin unlocks. Choose a skin from the dropdown.
  • Gold slice: Splitting a gold‑colored slice gives 50 pts (instead of 10) and the slice disappears.
